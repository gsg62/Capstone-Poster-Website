Biosphere

Preserving tropical forest biodiversity: an app to inform policy makers

Our Project:

The Biosphere capstone team aims to create a progressive web application, that provides an efficient and easy-to-use interface for the Madingley model server. The application will be created using the most modern development technologies and practices, and will be compatible with both iOS and Android platforms.

Our Team

Our team consists of four NAU Computer Science Undergraduates, our sponsor NAU School of Informatics, Computing, and Cyber Systems (SICCS) Professor Chris Doughty, and our mentor NAU SICCS graduate student Andrew Abraham.
